DESTINATION

The Travel Recommendation App helps users explore popular travel destinations through an interactive and user-friendly interface. It allows users to search for beaches, temples, countries, or specific country names to view related cities along with images and descriptions. The app dynamically fetches data from a structured JSON file to display relevant results. It provides a simple and efficient way to discover and plan travel destinations.
